// BIO

Brewing within the vibrant landscape of Tbilisi a creative force has been forging its path in the realm of electronic music. DMD is the new project of Luka Agulashvili. Hailing from Tbilisi, Georgia, this multifaceted artist has been making waves since his emergence in 2015 with his unique blend of minimal techno and microhouse.

Drawing from his classical music background, DMD's productions seamlessly fuse intricate melodies with infectious beats, exploring darker, more experimental soundscapes and grooves.

Under his original, Agoosh moniker, Luka explores the deeper & melodic side of house music, taking inspiration from Chicago house music pioneers. His upbringing into music theory and composition allows him to transcend genre boundaries, continually pushing the limits of electronic music.

With each passing year, DMD's repertoire grows more refined and expansive, reflecting his relentless drive for artistic growth and experimentation. This commitment to innovation can be seen in his mixes & live acts.
